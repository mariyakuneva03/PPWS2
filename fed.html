For these challenges I did some research, because they were a bit confusing at first. I was watching YouTube videos and looking at examples in W3Schools and Codepen. Level 1 Challenges Code Folder
Clock (For the clock I used div for every number on the clock and translate:rotate(); property on every number so I can position them in a circle around the middle of the clock. The 3 hands of the clock are updating based on the real time. For that I used JavaScript function that gets the seconds, minutes and the hours from the current time and rotates the hands based on the values it gets from the time.)
Accordion(For the accordion I used function, which on click changes the class of the parrent div of the heading that you click on (from "accordionItemHeading" to either "accordionItemHeading open" or "accordionItemHeading close") so the content shows or hides.)
